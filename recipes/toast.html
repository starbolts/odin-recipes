Toast

This is a simple toast recipe, very cozy and easy to make!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Pieces of White Bread
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Heat 2 pieces of white bread in toaster
  2. Melt butter in microwave on low for 30 seconds
  3. Spread butter across toasted bread slices
  4. Evenly sprinkle garlic powder onto buttered toast slices
